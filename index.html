ScoreBoard

ScoreBoard is an Android app for recording details about board gaming groups, including group members, group details and the games played by the group, including their outcomes. These details are then used to calculate monthly scores and member skill ratings. Skill ratings are calculated using a variation on the Elo rating system that can assign rating changes in games with more than 2 players or teams.

I created everything in this app.
